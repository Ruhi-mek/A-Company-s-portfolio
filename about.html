Our Story

Founder's Journey

Zenith Wellness was founded by Dr. Sarah Mitchell, a licensed psychologist who experienced burnout during her corporate career. After discovering the transformative power of mindfulness and therapy, she dedicated her life to making mental wellness accessible to everyone.

Our mission is to provide professional, compassionate mental health support to busy professionals and students who struggle with stress, anxiety, and burnout.
