Is my information kept private Most frequently asked by customers

Yes. We use industry-standard encryption and strict privacy policies. Your sessions and personal data are confidential and never shared without your consent.

How do online sessions work

Sessions are held via secure video call. You book a time, receive a link, and join from any device with internet. Same quality as in-person therapy.

Can I choose my therapist

Yes. You can browse our therapists, read their profiles, and request a session with the one who best fits your needs.

Do you offer emergency support

We are not an emergency service. If you are in crisis, please contact your local emergency number or a crisis helpline. We can support ongoing care.
