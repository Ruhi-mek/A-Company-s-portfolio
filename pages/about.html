About Us

Our founder's story

Zenith Wellness was founded by Dr. Salihat Mohammed, a psychologist who overcame burnout through mindfulness and therapy. She partnered with Ruhama Mekonen, a former tech executive who found balance after struggling with anxiety. Together, they created Zenith Wellness to make mental health tools simple, practical, and accessible for everyone.

mission
our mission is to provide professional, compassionate mental health support to busy professionals and students who struggle with stress, anxiety, and burnout.
